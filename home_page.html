Art

One of my hobbies (before all of my time was dominated by my university courses) was art. Although I experimented with a lot of different mediums, my favourite is ink and paper. This is probably due to a combination of its simplicity and the fact that I'm hopeless with a paintbrush. Feel free to have a look at some of my stuff!

Photography

My photography tends to be a bit more successful than my art. Most likely as it's a lot easier! I have had the opportunity to shoot number 1 UK artists, events, models and, of course, my family and friends. I use both film and digital, with no strict preference, although I favour film for personal pictures and digital for any work.
