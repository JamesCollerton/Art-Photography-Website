Hannah

These are some shots I took of Hannah for my portfolio. Previous to now I hadn't had much experience working with people in this context, so it was good to have a friend who could help out!

Half of the challenge was the technical taking of the photos, but most of it centers around getting people to relax!

We started with a few outdoors shots and then moved onto some more studio-style ones. Studio is much easier as you have much more control over light etc.

Laura

Laura let me take a few shots for my portfolio as well. Although I experimented with film when we were out I think all of my favourites I did with digital.

The uni lets me borrow equipment so I had access to some good flashes and diffusers. This was shot with a fisheye lens as well, hence the picture's curvature.

We started with a few outdoors shots and then moved onto some more studio-style ones. Studio is much easier as you have much more control over light etc.
